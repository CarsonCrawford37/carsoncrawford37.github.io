BIO

Sean McKenzie is the president of LawToys Inc., an umbrella company that designs, manufactures, and sells "innovative, high quality, and most advanced video game systems." He has been called "the current Father of the PC platform for kids". Due to the seriousness of many of his games, the company employs security guards for when the games are played in public. McKenzie also published "Video Games", a 2003 book about video games, some of which are based on LawToys products. He has also done advertising work for Marvel Comics and Guinness. McKenzie was a judge for the 6th Annual Independent Games Festival in 2008. He also did consulting for UbiSoft in 2010.
