XR Development Projects

From the moment I received my first Oculus Rift CV1 in high school, my passion for working with virtual reality and augmented reality ignited. I was determined to create immersive experiences that would captivate and inspire. My first VR program was for my high school Senior Project, where I developed a VR driving simulation aimed at equipping new student drivers with the skills to navigate hazardous situations confidently.
It wasn't until later on in college when I learned how to develop with augmented reality. My first app was a mini-golf game, leveraging image anchoring technology to offer users an experience filled with fun and challenging golfing adventures

Skills: AR Foundation, C#, OVR, Reality Composer, Unity 3D, XCode, XR Toolkit
