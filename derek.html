Derek Pyatt

Bio:

Derek Pyatt is a world-renowned Astrophysicist that has many otherworldly discoveries under his belt including the discovery of the moon and the invention of Jupiter. Pyatt also ventured into business starting his own company called "CVS Pharmacy" in 1963 after his syntheses of the now widely used drug called "Benadryl". Pyatt is also known for his impressive acting career that started with his lead role as Batman in the Christian Bale trilogy all the way to becoming an extra on the second Pacific Rim film. Pyatt's not done yet though, he plans to be the first human to every consume over 12 hotdogs in one sitting.
