Game Development Projects

Get Milky

Centering around the idea of an accessibility-focused game, Get Milky is a local multiplayer game made in Unity3D and C#. The goal for this project was to make it as inclusive as possible, meaning making customizable settings as well as accessible controllers.

Learn more

Magi VR

Magi VR is a puzzle, spell-casting, dungeon crawler that implements voice recognition for a unique and mysterious experience. This was a solo development project to further expand my knowledge of VR and game development.

Learn more

Finger Gun: Showdown

Being my first project developed in Unreal Engine 5, Finger Gun: Showdown is a free-for-all, multiplayer, first person shooter, allowing you to fight your friends and enemy NPCs alike. With only an 8-week deadline, I worked closely in a team of four to rapidly learn, prototype, and develop to successfully create our game.
